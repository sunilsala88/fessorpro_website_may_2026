Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of experienced quantitative analysts and finance professionals, FessorPro emerged from the recognition that traditional financial education often falls short of preparing students for the rapidly evolving landscape of modern finance.

Our founders, having worked at leading financial institutions including Goldman Sachs, Deutsche Bank, and Bank of America, identified a critical gap in the market: the lack of practical, hands-on training in Python programming for finance and algorithmic trading strategies.

Our Expertise

Our team consists of seasoned professionals with decades of combined experience across key areas of quantitative finance:

✦ Algorithmic Trading Strategy Development
✦ Python Programming for Finance
✦ Machine Learning in Financial Markets
✦ Options Trading and Derivatives
