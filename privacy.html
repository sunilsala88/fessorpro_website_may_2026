Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information on our platform.

📅 Last Updated: May 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") governs the manner in which the Platform collects, uses, maintains and discloses information of its users. The Policy also describes the practices that We apply to such user information, user's privacy rights and choices regarding their information. This Policy applies to all users of the Platform (referred to as "Learners", "You", "Your").

Important: By accessing and using the Platform, providing Your Personal Information, or by otherwise signalling Your agreement when the option is presented to You, You consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of information described in this Policy and Terms of Use.

1Personal Information

"Personal Information" shall mean information which identifies a Learner — first and last name, identification number, email address, age, gender, location, photograph and/or phone number provided at the time of registration or any time thereafter on the Platform.

"Sensitive Personal Information" shall include passwords and financial data, health data, official identifiers (such as biometric data, Aadhar number, social security number, driver's license, passport, etc.), information about sexual life, sexual identifier, race, ethnicity, political or religious belief or affiliation, account details and passwords, or other data categorized as 'sensitive personal data' under applicable Data Protection Laws.

2Information We Collect

We may collect both personal and non-personal identifiable information from You in a variety of ways, including when You visit our Platform, register on the Platform, and in connection with other activities, services, features or resources we make available. Please note that:

  • • We do not ask You for Personal Information unless we truly need it, like when You are registering for any Content on the Platform.
  • • We do not share Your Personal Information with anyone except to comply with applicable laws, develop our products and services, or protect our rights.
  • • We do not store Personal Information on our servers unless required for the on-going operation of our Platform.

Personal Identifiable Information: We may collect personal-identifiable information such as Your name and email address to enable Your access to the Platform and services/products offered therein. You can always refuse to provide such information; however, it may prevent You from accessing services or products provided on the Platform.

3How We Use and Share the Information Collected

We may collect and use Your Personal Information for the following purposes:

To provide access to our Platform

We use Your information to allow You to access the Platform and the services/products offered therein, including to provide customer service, fulfil purchases through the Platform, verify User information and to resolve any glitches. The legal basis for this processing is consent or, where applicable, our legitimate interests in the proper administration of our Platform.

To improve our Platform and maintain safety

We use Your information to improve and customize the Platform. Further, we use Your information to prevent, detect, investigate, and take measures against criminal activity, fraud, misuse of or damage to our Platform or network, and other threats and violations.

4Your Choices

  • • Limit the information You provide: You always have an option to choose the information You provide to us, including the option to update or delete Your information.
  • • Limit the communications You receive from us: You will have the option to choose what kind of communication You would like to receive. However, there may be certain communications required for legal or security purposes that you may not be able to limit.
  • • Reject Cookies: You may reject or remove cookies from Your web browser. However, some services offered on the Platform may not function when cookies are disabled.

5Your Rights

  • • Right to Confirmation and Access: You have the right to get confirmation and access to your Personal Information along with other supporting information.
  • • Right to Correction: You have the right to ask us to rectify your Personal Information that you think is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • • Right to be Forgotten: You have the right to restrict or prevent the continuing disclosure of your Personal Information under certain circumstances.

6Protection of Your Information

We take all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of Personal Information. Our disclosure of any such information is limited to our employees and contractors who need to know that information, and in response to court orders or governmental requests.

Your information including any Personal Information is stored, processed, and transferred in and to the Amazon Web Service (AWS) servers and databases located in India. We may also store, process, and transfer information in and to servers in other countries depending on the location of our affiliates and service providers.

If you use our Platform from outside India, including in the USA, EU, EEA, and UK, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in India. By accessing our Platform, you consent to the transfer of information to India and other countries outside your country of residence.

9Duration for Which Your Information is Stored

We will retain Your information for as long as it is required for the purposes stated herein, including for the purpose of complying with legal obligations or business compliances. We will never disclose aggregated or de-identified information in a manner that could identify you as an individual.
